Welcome to APM Infratech

Leading the Way in Heavy Engineering, Facility Management, and Security Services Across India.

APM INFRATECH has been into business of heavy engineering, facility management and security services since 2017, operating in north, west and south India. Our business is characterized by professionalism and high standards of service and client satisfaction. Organic growth is integrated into our long-term strategy. APM Infratech has not only mastered the art of fabrication and erection but has also diversified into manpower handling and onsite security services
Infrastructure, maintenance and safety of a country be it roads, dams, trains , dams etc are key parameters to judge the growth of a country. APM is aligning with the country’s vision of achieving this growth. APM is productively contributing by leveraging its execution process and engineering strengths.

Our mission is to deliver world-class construction and infrastructure solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental sustainability.
