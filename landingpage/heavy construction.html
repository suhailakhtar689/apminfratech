We are into mechanical and civil works pertaining to infra projects of the country. Primarily our teams are working at various metro rail projects, bullet train projects and railways bridge projects. APM Infratech has not only mastered the art of fabrication and erection but has also diversified into man power contracts and civil works. Infrastructure of a country be it roads, dams, trains etc are key parameters to judge the growth of a country. APM is aligning with the country’s vision of achieving this growth. APM is committed to deliver highest quality work on time to ensure client satisfaction.
